Policy

Reviews policy

We aim for reviews that are fair, helpful, and based on real experiences. We moderate submissions to reduce spam and abuse.

What we accept

  • First-hand experiences with an instructor (lesson, package, test prep).
  • Details that help others: suburb, auto/manual, lesson style, punctuality.

What we remove

  • Hate speech, threats, harassment, or personal information.
  • Defamation risks, unverified criminal allegations, or doxxing.
  • Spam, fake reviews, or incentives that aren’t disclosed.

Sponsored listings

Some placements may be paid. These are clearly labelled as Sponsored.

Disputes

If you’re an instructor and believe a review is inaccurate, contact us with evidence and we’ll investigate.
